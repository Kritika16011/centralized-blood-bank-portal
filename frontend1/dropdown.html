Welcome to the Central Blood Bank

Our mission is to ensure a safe and adequate blood supply for those in need. Join us in saving lives by donating blood, volunteering, or organizing blood drives.

We are committed to ensuring a constant and reliable supply of blood for patients in need. Our blood bank serves hospitals and clinics, providing blood for surgeries, trauma care, and emergency situations.
