1. What is E-RAKT PORTAL?

E-RAKT PORATAL is a centralized blood bank management system designed to ensure the proper collection, storage, and distribution of blood and its components.

2. How can I register as a blood donor?

You can register as a blood donor by visiting the E-RAKT PORTAL portal and filling out the donor registration form under the "Donor" section.

3. How does E-RAKT PORTAL help patients?

E-RAKT PORTAL connects patients with blood banks that have the required blood type in stock, ensuring quick access to blood in emergencies.

4. Is my personal information secure on E-RAKT PORTAL?

Yes, E-RAKT PORTAL prioritizes data security and ensures that all personal information is stored securely and used only for authorized purposes.

5. Can I search for specific blood groups on the portal?

Yes, E-RAKT PORTAL allows you to search for specific blood groups in registered blood banks across the area.

6. How do I locate the nearest blood bank?

You can use the "Find Blood Bank" feature on the E-RAKT PORTAL portal to locate the nearest blood bank based on your location.

7. Is there any fee for blood donation?

No, donating blood is a voluntary act, and no fees are charged for blood donation.

8. Can hospitals register on E-RAKT PORTAL?

Yes, hospitals can register on E-RAKT PORTAL to request blood units for their patients.

9. How can I check my blood donation history?

Once registered, you can log in to your account on E-RAKT PORTAL and view your donation history under the "My Donations" section.

10. What are the eligibility criteria for donating blood?

Donors must be aged between 18-65 years, weigh at least 50 kg, and be in good health at the time of donation.

11. How frequently can I donate blood?

Men can donate blood every 3 months, while women can donate every 4 months.

12. Can I donate blood if I have a medical condition?

It depends on the condition. Please consult a medical professional or contact the nearest blood bank for advice.

13. How does E-RAKT PORTAL ensure blood safety?

E-RAKT PORTAL ensures that all donated blood undergoes rigorous testing to meet safety and quality standards before distribution.

14. Can I request blood for an emergency through E-RAKT PORTAL?

Yes, you can submit an emergency blood request through the portal, and the system will guide you to the nearest blood bank with availability.

15. Who manages E-RAKT PORTAL?
